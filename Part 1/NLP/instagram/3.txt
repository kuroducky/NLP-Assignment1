Eddie Van Halen, the renowned lead guitarist of iconic rock group Van Halen, has died, according to a social media post by his son. He was 65. Van Halen learned to play the piano when he was six, formed his first band with his brother in the 4th grade and went on to become the eighth greatest guitarist of all-time, according to Rolling Stone magazine. "My whole life has been music. I could not imagine anything else," he told CNN in 2017. ?
